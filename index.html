Mobile Food Ordering App

Street Vendor

This food ordering app focuses on a gap in the market, street food. And by street food, I don't mean the trending food carts and truck pods concentrated in various major cities. I'm talking about, the entrepreneurial immigrant vendors that see a need and set up shop on the busy streets of every major city in the U.S. There is such a rich culture in these street vendor communities and the Street Vendor app aims to offer an insight into these culinary cultural experiences. Anyone, from a self proclaimed foodie, a busy worker looking for a quick bite, to a native of that city will be able to locate local food vendors in their area without having to rely on word of mouth or sheer luck.
